Help Info

What is this?

This app allows you to organize your time, thoughts, concerns, and progress in one place.

Date

Today's date should be shown by default. Change it if it's the wrong date.

Timer

  • Click START when you're ready to begin studying.

  • Taking a break? PAUSE it, then RESUME when you're ready back on it.

  • If for some reason you need to reset the time, click the reset icon underneath the button.

Questions

Jot down any materials or concept that is not clear to you or that you would like to re-visit at a later time. Use the list in your next mentor session, office hours, or next study time.

  • Pressing ENTER will make a new entry underneath.

  • Pressing UP or DOWN arrow key at the start or beginning will move you to the previous or next entry respectively.

GitHub Pushes

Updates your pushed commit history automatically every 3 minutes if the username is supplied. Click the Refresh icon to update manually. Note: It may take a minute or two before your recent pushed commit is reflected.

Notes

Don't loose what you've learned: describe important concepts in your own words in writting so you have a record of it.

Past Study Sessions

Track how much progress you've made and find ways to keep it up or improve it.
